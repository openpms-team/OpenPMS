## Overview

The analytics module provides real-time insights into your rental operation. Track key performance indicators, compare properties, and ask questions about your data using natural language.

## KPI Dashboard

The main dashboard displays cards for core metrics:

- **Occupancy rate** — Percentage of available nights booked
- **Average daily rate (ADR)** — Revenue per occupied night
- **Revenue per available room (RevPAR)** — ADR multiplied by occupancy
- **Total revenue** — Gross income for the period
- **Average stay length** — Mean nights per reservation
- **Cancellation rate** — Percentage of cancelled bookings

All KPIs support date range filtering (week, month, quarter, year, custom) and property filtering.

## Charts

Visual reports built with Recharts:

- Revenue over time (line chart)
- Occupancy by month (bar chart)
- Revenue by channel source (pie chart)
- Booking lead time distribution (histogram)
- Seasonal trends year-over-year (comparison line chart)

## Property Comparison

Select multiple properties to compare side-by-side on any metric. Useful for identifying underperforming units or validating pricing changes.

## AI BI Chat

Ask questions about your data in natural language:

- "What was my occupancy rate in July?"
- "Which property generated the most revenue last quarter?"
- "Show me the trend of average nightly rate over the past 12 months"

The AI translates your question into a database query, runs it, and presents the result as text or a chart. Requires an AI API key configured in Settings.
