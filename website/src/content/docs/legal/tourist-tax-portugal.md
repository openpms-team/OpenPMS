## Overview

Several Portuguese municipalities charge a **Taxa Municipal Turistica** (tourist tax) per guest per night. The tax is collected by the accommodation provider and remitted to the local authority. OpenPMS calculates and tracks this automatically.

## How It Works in OpenPMS

1. Configure the tourist tax rate for each property under its settings
2. When a reservation is created, the tax is calculated automatically
3. The tax amount appears on the reservation detail and in financial reports
4. Owner statements separate tourist tax from revenue (it is not income)

## Exemptions

The following guests are typically exempt from tourist tax:

- Children under 13 years old
- Guests with medical treatment appointments in the municipality
- Residents of the municipality

Exemption rules vary by municipality. OpenPMS lets you mark individual guests as exempt when creating or editing a reservation.

## Remittance

Tourist tax collected must be remitted to the municipality according to their schedule (typically monthly or quarterly). OpenPMS generates a report of all collected tax per period to simplify this process.
