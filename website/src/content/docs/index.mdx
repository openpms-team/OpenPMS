## Why OpenPMS?

Deploy your own property management system in under 10 minutes. No subscriptions, no vendor lock-in, no data hostage situations.

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Zero Cost Infrastructure" icon="star">
    Runs entirely on Vercel (free tier) and Supabase (free tier). No monthly fees, no per-booking charges.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI-Powered Operations" icon="rocket">
    Smart pricing, automated guest messaging, OCR check-in, BI chat, and more. Bring your own API key, control your budget.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Guest Self-Check-In" icon="approve-check">
    Mobile-friendly 4-step wizard with document OCR. Guests complete check-in before arrival.
  </Card>
  <Card title="SEF/SIBA Compliance" icon="document">
    Automatic XML generation for SEF bulletins and DAT files for SIBA. Built for Portuguese Alojamento Local.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Channel Sync via iCal" icon="translate">
    Import and export iCal feeds from Airbnb, Booking.com, and others. Conflict detection included.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Task Management" icon="list-format">
    Kanban boards, auto-generated cleaning tasks, mobile-optimized checklists for your team.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-Language" icon="open-book">
    Interface available in Portuguese, English, and French. Guest portal adapts to the guest's language.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Your Data, Your Server" icon="setting">
    Self-hosted on your own Supabase instance. Full database access, export anytime, no lock-in.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Built for Short-Term Rental Managers

OpenPMS covers the full lifecycle: properties, reservations, guest check-in, messaging, tasks, finances, owner reports, and analytics. Whether you manage 1 apartment or 50, the stack scales with you at zero cost.
